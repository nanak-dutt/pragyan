Sheik Customs is one of the gulf's leading and fast-growing automobile modification and repair organization. They help clients all over the world to transform and modify automobiles such as motorcycles and cars.

Specializing in both cars and bikes, and dealing with over 45 automotive brands they’ve won many competitions all around the gulf and have won awards in many global competitions such as Bahrain expo, SEMA, GCC Auto Award (2006), Middle East Auto Awards (2013 and 2018), USA Auto Awards (2012) and Auto World Award (2019)

With experienced airbrush artists who give cars and bikes an edgy new look with their artistic work with the best paint brands from the USA in their high-tech modern paint booth.

1) Does the workshop have any prerequisites?

Any student having a good internet connection can attend the workshop.

 

2) How should the payment be made for the workshop?

Payment can be made online on the pragyan.org website after creating an account and clicking on the "Registration" tab of the corresponding workshop.

3) On what basis are workshop registrations confirmed?

Registrations are confirmed after payment on a first come first serve basis. Confirmation of your participation is subject to availability.

 

4) Will the fees paid for the workshop be refunded in case I fail to attend the workshop?

Fees once paid is strictly non-refundable. Requests for a refund will not be entertained.

 

5) Will certificates be provided?

Yes. A certificate from Pragyan, NIT Trichy will be provided after the completion of the workshop through email. Another certificate will be provided to participants who were top performers in the workshop which will be decided by the involvement in the workshop quiz taken at the end of the workshop.
