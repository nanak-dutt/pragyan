Bracket

Valorant Gamescape Valorant


For all the gamers out there, Pragyan '21 presents the Gamescapes eSports Tournament with Valorant and CSGO competitions. Flaunt your gaming skills and win exciting prizes.
With each of us fighting our own battles amidst the pandemic, gaming has been a major factor in bringing people together. 

Event Dates: 29th March 2021 to 4th April 2021

Prizes worth INR 10K.

  • It is a group event, and every group is required to have exactly five members, with an optional sixth substitute member.

  • Players can not be replaced once the match starts.

  • All players are allowed to use only one account per person.

  • All cheats are completely forbidden to use under any circumstance. Using any cheat will result in disqualification of the whole team.

  • Teams will be selected to participate in knockouts on a first-come, first-serve basis.

  • In-game rules will be informed to the teams post-registration.

  • Decision made by the organizers will be final

  • Knockouts will be conducted to shortlist the top 8 teams to the quarterfinals.

  • The top four teams from the quarterfinals will reach the semi-finals, where the top two teams will reach the finals.

  • Both CS:GO and Valorant will have custom matches based on Competitive Mode

  • The semifinal and final matches will be streamed online live.
