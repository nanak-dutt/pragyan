• Prosthesis

Jonathan Tippett, from Vancouver, Canada, has always been fascinated by the dynamic between humans and machines. This is what led him to design and build the largest tetrapod exo-skeleton, which he named “Prosthesis”.

The exo-skeleton’s body and legs are made from 1,600 kg (3,527 lb) of Chromoly steel tubing, a high-performance material typically used in the aerospace and racing car industry. Check out the exhibit below!

              

  • Julian Voss-Andreae

With pioneering art pieces such as ‘Foamball’ and ‘Elective Affinities’, German sculptor Julian Voss-Andreae is able to effortlessly blend fields like painting, sculpting, and physics seamlessly. His sculptures are frequently shown at international art fairs and galleries and can be found in major collections in North America, Asia, Europe, Africa, and Australia.

              

  • Ordnance Factory Tiruchirappalli

Ordnance Factory Tiruchirappalli (OFT) is India's largest small arms manufacturing company. Often called the fourth arm of the defence force, OFT is the epitome of the make-in-India movement, employing the latest and sophisticated weapons and small arms manufacturing technology.
