  • Rules

  • It is a group event, and every group is required to have exactly five members, with an optional sixth substitute member.

  • Players can not be replaced once the match starts.

  • All players are allowed to use only one account per person.

  • All cheats are completely forbidden to use under any circumstance. Using any cheat will result in disqualification of the whole team.

  • Teams will be selected to participate in knockouts on a first-come, first-serve basis.

  • In-game rules will be informed to the teams post-registration.

  • Decision made by the organizers will be final
