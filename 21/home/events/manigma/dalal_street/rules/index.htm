Rules

  • All players are given a fixed amount of cash when they start. 

  • Players make/lose money by trading stocks at the exchange. 

  • Players get to predict market behaviour by virtue of the constant news feed. 

  • The market will often swing wildly - keep a keen eye on the news. 

  • A constantly updating market depth will be shown for each stock to help players decide what other players think of that stock. 

  • There will be a score called ‘reputation’, which is indicative of their behaviour during the game, and the player might receive negative points if it goes too low. 

  • Phone Verification is used for the identification of unique players. 

  • Users can watch over a particular stock and get notifications for significant changes in stock. 

  • Users can refer the game to others and get in-game cash rewards

 

Judging Criteria 

The Net Worth of the players at the end of the game will be the key judging criterion. (Net Worth = Cash in hand + Worth of stocks)
