Description

Beer Factory is an online strategy-based game designed to test the skill of the participants in the art of resource management. There are two different game modes available for the participants: Single Player mode and Duel mode. 

Participants are expected to manage the production and management processes in a beer factory and make a sincere attempt to maximize the factory’s profit to gain popularity. In the Single Player game mode, participants compete against a computer-simulated opponent.
