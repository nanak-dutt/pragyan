Description

Pragyan Premier League is an online fantasy cricket league that lets you manage your own team. From buying your squad to deciding the line-ups, your decisions decide the fate of your team. Offering a dynamic environment in which you have to compete with managers worldwide in real-time, the event forces you to strategise and master the odds to outwit your opponent. 

 

Eight days. Eight matches. One team. Do you have what it takes to lead your team to glory?

Event is live now at: ppl.pragyan.org

Date of Start :  5th April 2021

Prize Money: INR 18000.
