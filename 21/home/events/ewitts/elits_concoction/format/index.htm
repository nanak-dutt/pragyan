Format

  • The event consists of three rounds:

    • First round: Speaking events

Participants must speak about a given topic on the spot. 

  • Extempore

  • JAM

  • Second round: What’s the good word

A combination of different word games, e.g., Spooners, Addagrams, etc.

  • Third round: Puzzle champ

It consists of different brain teaser challenges.
