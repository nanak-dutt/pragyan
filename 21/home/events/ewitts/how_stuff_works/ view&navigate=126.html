Ever wondered why things are the way they are? Does the working and reason behind the existence of things make your mind agog? Then, buckle up, for you will be tested on how you perceive and understand everything, and by everything, we mean everything. Come, participate in the How Stuff Works Quiz, and win an exciting prize.

EVENT IS COMPLETED

Prizes worth: INR 10000

 

  • Prelims consist of a team quiz (written) with a maximum of two members in a team, which will happen on the zoom platform.

  • Finals consist of themed rounds (written) and regular rounds (bounce/pounce format) conducted on the zoom platform.

  • Teams of two.

  • Written Prelims

  • Top 8-10 teams qualify for the finals.

  • Cheating/misconduct by any means will lead to disqualification. 

 

Judging criteria:

  • The top 8-10 teams from the prelims qualify for the finals.

  • The format of judging and scoring will be revealed during the event.

F.A.Q:

Question: What is HSW?

Ans: HSW stands for How Stuff Works, and as the name suggests, it deals with the working of any device or phenomenon from any field.

 

Question: How do I participate?

Ans: All you need to do is register for the event on the Pragyan website and show your Pragyan ID at the event’s commencement.
