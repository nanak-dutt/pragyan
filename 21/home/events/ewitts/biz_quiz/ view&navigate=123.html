The Biz Quiz, PRAGYAN, NITT, is an event to test the business knowledge of participants. Stretching across different business-related topics, the quiz will be a test of not just knowledge but also the lateral thinking skills of the participants.

EVENT IS DONE

PRIZE MONEY: INR 10000

 

  • There will be two rounds, namely prelims and finals.

  • Prelims will be an online round that will be conducted on Zoom.

  • Top 8-10 teams will qualify for the finals.

  • Finals will also be conducted on ZOOM in regular rounds (bounce/pounce format).



 

 

  • Maximum two members will be allowed in a team.

  • Any malpractice during the quiz will lead to direct disqualification.

 

FAQs:

 

1) What is Biz Quiz?

This quiz covers various topics of business, companies, and related things.

 

2) Who can participate in Biz Quiz?

Anyone can participate in this event after their registration on the Pragyan website.
