Rules

- It is an individual event.

- In Round 2, only C and C++ languages are allowed.

- Do not discuss strategy, suggestions, or post solutions or tips in the comments during a live contest. Questions clarifying the problem statement must be asked directly to the organisers.

- Involvement in any kind of plagiarism or malpractice will lead to disqualification.

- In any situation, the decision of the organizers is final and binding.

 

Judging Criteria :

     Round 1:

  • Users will be ranked according to the number of problems solved.

  • Ties will be broken by the total time taken.

     Round 2:

  • The solution has to pass all the test cases first.

  • Each problem will be judged on the length of the source code. The best solution, i.e., the shortest solution, will receive the full score, and all the other solutions will receive a fraction based on how close they come to the best one.

      Round 3:

  • The candidate who solves first can proceed to the next challenge.

  • In case of a tie, both the participants from that knockout round will proceed to the next challenge.
