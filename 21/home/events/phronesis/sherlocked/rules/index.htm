Rules

  • The first round will be an individual round. Based on the scores, teams will be formed by the Event organizers, and a discord channel for the same will be made.

  • Any kind of malpractices during the event is strictly prohibited.

  • The decision of the event managers will be deemed final.

 

 

Judging Criteria:

 

Round 1:

  • Players are ranked in order of the points they score.

  • In case of ties, the participant who has completed the quiz in the least time will be given priority.

Round 2 and 3:

  • Finding the exact incident at the crime scene - 20 points

  • Finding relevant clues in the crime scene   - 10 points

  • Using an extra clue 1 would cost : (-5) points
  • Using an extra clue 2 would cost : (-15) points
  • Using an extra clue 3 would cost : (-30) points

 

The aggregate of the score will be taken for each description, and the best answer will be chosen among the given three solutions for every team.

In case of a tie, the time of answering will be taken as judging criteria.
